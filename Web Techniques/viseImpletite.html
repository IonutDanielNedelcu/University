Vise Împletite cu Vio și Clau
(eng. Braided Dreams with Vio and Clau)

"Our goal was and is to help more people find these (knitting and crocheting) hobbies. We managed to gather, until now, a community of over 42 thousand wonderful souls, passioned about handmade, a number big enough to fill the Grand Arena stadium from Cluj.

      Three years ago we started writing crocheting and knitting patterns. Since then we created over 100 patterns, varying in difficulty from begginer to advanced. We create these patterns monthly, both for women, men, and children (we are the only ones in Romania)."

Violeta Prisăcaru and Claudia Popliceanu

Violeta Prisăcaru has a master's degree in contability and worked for eight years as senior insurance broker. Then, she decided to pursue her passion and graduated from fashion design courses, ready to take her career completely in her own hands. You can find her YouTube channel here.

      Claudia Popliceanu worked in press, as news editor - Economic Section. After she became a mother, she gave up journalism and went back to her first love, photography, discovering at the same time her love for handcraft.You can find her YouTube channel here.

Weekly Broadcast

Their weekly YouTube broadcast grew over the last four years and reached the fourth season, being watched live by hundreds of people from Romania and not only. Their show got to people from the Republic of Moldova, and romanians from all over the Europe and America (Germany, UK, USA, Canada, France, Italy, Norway, Sweden, Austria, many more).
