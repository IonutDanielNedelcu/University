Prima Dragoste

The collaboration with Prima Dragoste is one of my best challenges. Usually working with dark colours, black and grey, I now have the opportunity to explore and play with whites and tans, with ligher or desaturated colours, but also reds. Her eclectic perspective offers the perfect counter-balance Nidle needs to create garments for her.

      Alexandra Cebotar has a repeating "bride" motif in her songs, setting white as the first colour option when it comes to stepping on stage.

Our collaboration is the thing behind the colours of the Monstera Earrings.

      As previously mentioned, Prima Dragoste is all about white and purity. As I firstly crafted the Monstera Earrings in their traditional colour, she thought they would look great in other colours, too, and she was not wrong!       Alexandra Cebotar is the drill when it comes to mixing and combining different styles, about reusing clothes and accessories in completely different outfits, and, as I like to say: She knows what she's doing.
