Collaborations - Small Talk

Nidle already had a few opportunities, and missed none of them. Collaborating with artists and other brands, Nidle's vision does not stop here. The perfect word to describe what Nidle does is "individual".

      Individual pieces (Nidle only creates limited edition products that you may never see again), individual characters (Nidle focuses on the personality and perspective of the artist or the collaborator), individual work (Nidle means me, and I mean Nidle. We are one and the same, the only difference is the perspective).

Dora Gaitanovici

Dora Gaitanovici is a young, daring and complex musical project. After a viral episode on DWMT and participation in Eurovision, as well as the release of a concept album and national tours, Dora Gaitanovici managed to impress with electrifying performances at the biggest festivals in Romania, becoming the detached leaders of the new alternative wave in the country.

      Their sound combines elements of alternative, progressive and modern rock with slight folk influences. Dora's captivating voice, Dante's guitar virtuosity, Dani's harmonies and Codruț's groove, together with the complex productions and the energy transmitted in each live, define the Dora Gaitanovici project.

Prima Dragoste

Prima Dragoste (eng. First Love), by her real name Alexandra Cebotar, is a Moldavian singer and songwriter. In high school, she studied piano and violin, period during which she composed her first song, "Scrisoare". A few months later, the second song "Portrete" followed; a piece that stood out in a larger audience thanks to it being featured in "Poliția muzicii", an online show created by Sebastian Coțofană.

      "Portrete" also reached the ears of the producers from Roton Music studios, who proposed Alexandra to take part in a recording session. Prima Dragoste emerged naturally as a way of expressing herself through music inspired by her own emotions and experiences.

Vise Împletite cu Vio și Clau

Vise Împletite cu Vio și Clau (Braided Dreams with Vio and Clau) is a romanian project made out of love for handcraft, it's target being to unite people of all ages under the umbrella of creativity.

      This project incorporates a weekly online show on Friday, monthly physical meetings in many Romanian cities, an annual country-level meeting that takes place in Bucharest, and many other events. It's appearance on Pro TV did not only offer a broader public, but also a symbol of recognition of manual arts.
